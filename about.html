F1Fans

Welcome to the About Us page! We are a small group of Formula 1 fans. who love everything about the sport. From the DRS to the frontwing, we want to know everything about it. Through the years the sport went so good we decided to make a F1 fanpage. Since Max Verstappen joined Formula 1 we are active on every race. From FP1 to qualifying to the race we are straight on it! Feel free to browse our Website and if you have any type of questions you can ask them on our contact page! Get ready and race on!
